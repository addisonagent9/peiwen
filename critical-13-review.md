## 徘
- **Ours**: 平 九佳 (default)
- **charles**: 平 十灰
- **jkak**: 平 九佳
- **cope**: 平 十灰
- **Consensus**: 平 十灰 (charles + cope)
- **Notes**: Modern Mandarin: pái. Used in 徘徊 (páihuái). All sources agree on 平, only the rhyme group differs. Charles and cope say 十灰; jkak agrees with us on 九佳. This is a rhyme-group disagreement, not a tone error. 十灰 (-āi final) aligns better with the modern pronunciation pái. Our 九佳 assignment may be wrong.

## 圯

- **jkak**: 平 四支
- **cope**: 仄 四紙
